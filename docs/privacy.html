Privacy Policy

How Forever Bets collects, uses, and protects information.

Overview

Forever Bets is a wedding predictions app where hosts create events and questions, guests submit predictions, and points are awarded on a leaderboard.

This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: the email address you use to sign in.
  • Event content: events you create, including titles, dates/times, locations, and related settings.
  • Predictions & participation: guest entries, predictions, and related scoring/leaderboard data.
  • Support communications: messages you send to support (and any details you include).

How We Use Information

  • To operate the app (create events, submit predictions, calculate scores, show leaderboards).
  • To provide support and troubleshoot issues.
  • To improve app reliability and user experience.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to provide the service (for example, hosting and infrastructure providers) or if required by law.

Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed to provide the service. You can delete your account at any time, which removes your account and the data you created in the app.

Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account directly in the app:

Admin → Account / Advanced → Delete My Account

If you can’t access the app, contact support at todgack69@gmail.com.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to maintain safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data.

Children’s Privacy

Forever Bets is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact support so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date when changes are made.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email todgack69@gmail.com.
