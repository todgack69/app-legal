Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-17

Overview

Wedding Wagers is an event-based predictions game for private groups (e.g., wedding guests).

Information we collect

  • Account/event information you provide (e.g., event title, guest display name).
  • App usage data needed to run the service (e.g., predictions, submissions, scores).

How we use information

  • To operate the app (create events, invite guests, collect predictions, show leaderboards).
  • To provide support and troubleshoot issues.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as needed to operate the service or comply with legal obligations.

Data retention

Data is kept as long as needed for app functionality. Hosts can delete data from within the app.
