Account & Data Deletion In-App

You can delete your Forever Bets account directly inside the app. This permanently deletes your account and the data you created in the app.

How to delete your account:
Admin → Account / Advanced → Delete My Account

If you can’t access the app, email support and include the email you used to sign in.
